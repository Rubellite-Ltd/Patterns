Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 13 September 2024

At Rubellite Ltd, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to all our mobile applications and services. By using our apps, you agree to the collection, use, and sharing of your information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: If you create an account or log in, we may collect your name, email address, and other details needed for authentication purposes.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our apps, such as features accessed, settings selected, and device information (including operating system and device type).
  • Location Data: Some features may require access to location data to provide location-based services.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies or similar technologies to track your interaction with our apps and improve your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the functionality of our apps.
  • To process your login and authentication.
  • To communicate with you, including sending support responses or promotional updates.
  • To personalize your experience within the app.
  • To enhance app performance and usability.
  • For security purposes to prevent fraud or misuse.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to help operate our apps, such as:

  • FormKeep for contact form submissions.
  • Analytics Tools to track app performance and usage patterns.

WebKit: Our apps use the WebKit framework for secure web browsing when opening external web pages.

These third parties are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and must provide equal or greater privacy protection.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf (e.g., FormKeep).
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your data if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users and the public.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. This includes encryption, secure authentication processes, and secure data storage.

6. Children's Privacy

Our apps comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental consent. If your app is directed at children, we do not include third-party analytics or advertising.

7. User Consent and Rights

  • Consent: By using our apps, you agree to the collection and use of your data as outlined in this policy.
  • Account Deletion: You have the right to delete your account at any time, and we will remove all associated personal data.
  • Data Access and Correction: You may request access to or correction of your personal information.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. You can request the deletion of your data by contacting us.

9. Compliance with App Store Guidelines

We adhere to Apple's App Store Guidelines, including:

  • Mobile Device Management (MDM): If applicable, any MDM services provided by our apps will request user consent and comply with legal requirements.
  • WebKit Usage: We use WebKit for secure browsing within the app, ensuring a safe user experience.
  • Developer Code of Conduct: We treat our users with respect and avoid manipulative practices.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by updating the "Effective Date" and, where appropriate, provide additional notice (such as in-app notifications).
